About Us

At Nirmal PG, we prioritize creating a welcoming and homely environment for all residents.

At Nirmal PG, we understand the importance of a comfortable and affordable living space for students and working professionals.
Our guest rental service is dedicated to providing cozy rooms that cater to single, double and triple occupancy, ensuring a welcoming environment that feels like home. With a focus on cleanliness and security, Nirmal PG offers a seamless stay that prioritizes your comfort and convenience.
